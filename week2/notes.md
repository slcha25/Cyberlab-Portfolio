## What I Learned

- In S5 Lab, I learned how to perform binary transformation, identify the "jump size" (block size) of any CIDR notation from /24 to /32 to determine network boundaries, use ipcalc to verify if a specific host IP falls within a logical subnet or is "out-of-bounds, and remediate CIDR Mismatches
